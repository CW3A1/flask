DESIGN IN THE CLOUD MATHEMATICAL SIMULATIONS FROM ANYWHERE

TRY IT

This site lets you do complex mathematical calculations and simulations, that are too difficult for the average computer, on our servers. This makes it possible to do these calculations from any device anywhere in the world, as long as you are connected to the internet. Our current possible simulations are differentiation, integration, 3D optimization, Lagrange interpolation, Taylor approximation and heat equation.

How? Why? Learn more >>

This site is the product of a student team project of the KU Leuven. It's a part of the subject P&O 3 where 6 bachelor engineer students work on a semester project they can choose. This one was designed and monitored by the department of computer science.
